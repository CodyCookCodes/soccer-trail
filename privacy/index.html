Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 4, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Oakland Pro Soccer (d/b/a Oakland Roots SC and Oakland Soul SC), Oakland 68s, Oaklandish, Oakland Ballers, Run Local Ventures LLC (d/b/a Oakland Marathon), and ("we," "us," or "our") operate an event-listing website (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information, and your rights under applicable laws including the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Other submitted information

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address
  • Device type, browser, operating system
  • Pages viewed, links clicked, referring URLs
  • Approximate location (via IP)

c. Communications & Consent Data

  • Email subscription status
  • SMS opt-in/opt-out status and timestamps
  • Records of consent (including form language shown at signup)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide and improve event listings and recommendations
  • Send newsletters, alerts, and promotional content
  • Send SMS messages (only with your explicit consent)
  • Personalize content and advertising
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. SMS Communications

If you opt in to receive SMS messages:

  • You agree to receive recurring automated promotional and informational text messages about events and updates
  • Message frequency may vary
  • Message and data rates may apply
  • You can opt out anytime by replying STOP
  • For help, reply HELP or contact info@rootssc.com

We maintain records of your consent in compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information for money. However, we may share personal information in the following ways:

a. Service Providers

We share data with vendors who help operate our business (e.g., hosting, email delivery, SMS providers, analytics platforms).

b. Advertising & Analytics Partners

We may share identifiers (such as cookies, device IDs, or hashed email) with advertising partners for purposes of targeted advertising and performance measurement. Under California law, this may be considered "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising.

c. Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

d. Business Transfers

Information may be transferred in connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to understand user behavior, improve the Site, and deliver relevant advertisements. These may include tools such as Google Analytics and Meta Pixel. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information"

California residents have the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information under the California Privacy Rights Act. While we do not sell personal information for monetary compensation, we may share data for advertising purposes that could be considered "sharing."

You may opt out by contacting us at info@rootssc.com. We will honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals where required.

8. Your Privacy Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act and CPRA, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Access a copy of your personal information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Opt out of sale or sharing
  • Limit use of Sensitive Personal Information (if applicable)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise your rights, contact: info@rootssc.com. We may verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain business records, and comply with legal obligations. Retention periods vary depending on the type of data.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your personal information. However, no system is completely secure.

11. Children's Privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Third-Party Links

The Site may link to third-party websites or event pages. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised "Last Updated" date.
